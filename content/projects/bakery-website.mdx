A **content-forward** bakery site emphasizing photography, seasonal menus, and clear calls to action (order, visit, contact). Built for performance (static generation where possible), accessibility, and simple deployment.

## Highlights

- **Responsive layout** tuned for mobile visitors discovering the shop on the go.
- **Structured content** for menu sections and location details.
- **SEO-friendly** metadata and semantic HTML.
