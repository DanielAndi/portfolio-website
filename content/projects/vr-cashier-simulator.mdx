# Project Overview

> **🌐 Accessible VR Training**
> 
> The VR Cashier Simulator is an engaging virtual environment designed specifically for neurodivergent learners. Neurodivergence refers to various conditions that affect cognitive functioning, learning, attention, and mood, including autism spectrum disorder, ADHD, dyslexia, dyspraxia, and others.
> 
> This project creates a simulation tailored to the unique needs of neurodivergent learners, emphasizing focus and immersiveness through carefully designed environments and interactions.

## 🎯 Key Features

- **AI-Driven Environment:** Users interact with simulated social scenarios, playing the role of a cashier in a virtual store
- **Calm & Structured Design:** Japanese architecture-inspired main menu creates a peaceful learning environment
- **Interactive Tutorials:** Step-by-step guidance with floating instructional videos and background narration
- **Dynamic AI Customers:** AI selects random items from shelves for players to scan and bag
- **Restocking System:** Complete game cycle includes scanning, bagging, and restocking items
- **Accessibility Focus:** Visual and auditory guidance ensures players understand controls and objectives

# 🎮 User Experience

## 🏛️ Main Menu

The environment features Japanese architecture-inspired design, giving it a calm and structured feel. The space includes invisible barriers (colliders) keeping players within the design space. Front and center, there's the title of the game along with an icon representing the store.

The menu is simple, offering two buttons:
- **Initial Tutorial:** Control instructions and VR basics
- **Store Tutorial:** Cashier-specific training

When players first open the app, they automatically begin in the initial tutorial.

## 📚 Initial Tutorial

The initial tutorial keeps things straightforward with visual distinction—originally plain with a white background, now enhanced with a red floor for better visual clarity.

The tutorial starts with a video that walks players through the controls, explaining what each button on the controller does. We chose video format because it's easier for players, especially those new to VR, to understand controls through clear and visual demonstration. The video guides players step-by-step, ensuring they know how to interact with the environment before moving on to the next tutorial.

Once completed, players are automatically taken to the store tutorial.

## 🛒 Store Tutorial

In the store tutorial, players learn how to:
- **Scan and bag items:** Practice the core cashier skills
- **Interact with the scanning machine:** Use the built-in UI for starting and completing transactions
- **Restock shelves:** Return items to their designated spots after transactions

### Game Flow

1. **AI Customer Interaction:** The AI selects random items from the shelves based on a randomizer that pulls from the store's inventory
2. **Scanning & Bagging:** Players scan each item and place them in bags
3. **Transaction Completion:** Players use the scanning machine's UI to finalize transactions
4. **Customer Departure:** Once the transaction is complete, the AI customer leaves the store
5. **Restocking:** Items spawn inside designated boxes, and players must place them on highlighted spots on the shelves
6. **Completion:** After all items are restocked, players can either return to the main menu or restart the tutorial
